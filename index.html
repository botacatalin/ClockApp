Interactive Learning Clock

A simple hands-on clock designed to help children understand analog time by directly moving the clock hands.

  • Drag the short hand to change the hour
  • Drag the long hand to change the minutes
  • ⟲ resets the clock to 12:30
  • 5′ shows 5-minute helper values
  • 24 overlays inner 24-hour values
  • ◉ shows or hides the digital clock
  • ⚙ changes colors and clock face styles
Drag the short hour hand or long minute hand.
